About Google Trends Data

Data is sourced from Google Trends API and includes:

  • Topic: The search term or topic that is trending
  • Category: The topic's primary category (Entertainment, Shopping, or Pop Culture)
  • Type: Whether the trend is daily or weekly
  • Search Interest: A relative score (0-100) that indicates search volume
  • Change: Change in search interest over the time period
  • Region: Geographic region of the trend data (US)
